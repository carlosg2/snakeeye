About SnakeEye

SnakeEye is a motion sensor. It detects camera motion, and when the activity level reaches a certain threshold, the application window automatically pops up so you can see what's moving.

I created SnakeEye to keep an eye on my pet snake. He spends most of the time hiding, so when he comes out, I don't want to miss it!

Top

How to Use

Just attach a camera, select your activity threshold by clicking on the desired dot, and minimize the window. When the activity level reaches the specified threshold, the window will automatically restore itself (pop back up) to show you what's moving.

If you want to change the camera that SnakeEye uses, click on the camera button and select a different camera. New cameras are not auto-detected, so if you attach a new camera, you must click on the camera button for it to be detected.
